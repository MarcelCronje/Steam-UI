Detail 3D Mapping and Modelling

(TOPOGRAPHICAL – ARCHITECTURAL)

• Detailed and accurate site information in various instances is sometimes required by architects and engineering professionals to start with additional construction or to plan demolishing or upgrades of existing sites and infrastructure.

• Making use of state-of-the-art 3D technology and techniques, we capture as-is information and convert it into workable and manageable datasets to cater to a variety of specialists working in different departments and can output to almost any required software format.

• Strict quality control processes through all data captured in the field are followed by professionals before outputs are done to enable 3D modeling and draughting teams to extract critical information as needed to produce required reports, CAD models, or specific and sufficient project-related data.

• Existing topographical, architectural, structural, civil, and MEP detail can be captured in one go whilst on site with a very fast turnaround time.

3D Laser Scanning Technology Benefits Numerous Industries

3D laser scanning has the potential to reduce follow-up visits and maintenance on site, improve safety, and increase flexibility for future brownfield modifications

3D laser scanning an existing facility is the ability to provide physical data for asset management activities. This may include quantity surveys, asset documentation, condition monitoring, tank calibration, maintenance support, and intelligent plant modeling.

Laser scanning technology has been gaining popularity in numerous industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, entertainment, construction, and automobile.

PRIMARY ADVANTAGES OF 3D LASER SCANNING:

– Rapid measurement

– Reduced HSE risk

– Non-contact data capture

– Detailed documentation

– Cost & time savings

– Intelligent data Asset management

– Condition assessment
